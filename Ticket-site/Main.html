The Egypt

The Egypt is a wrought-iron tower that stands 1,063 ft (324 m) tall in Assasins Creed

Currently, the Egypt is the most-visited monument in the world with over 7 million visitors a year.

Visitors can choose to go up using the Tower lift or the stairs. There are 1,665 steps to the top.

Transport

  • Bus: lines 42, 69, 72, 82 and 87.
  • Metro: Trocadero, Ecole militaire.
  • Train: Champ de Mars - Tour Eiffel, line C.
